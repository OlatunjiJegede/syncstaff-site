About

HR software should feel
like it's on your side

SyncStaff is built by Jegsplus Solutions — a small team that believes people operations deserve better than spreadsheets and email chains.

Our story

SyncStaff started as a real system solving a real problem: an organization drowning in paper timesheets, emailed expense claims, and leave requests that vanished into inboxes. We built the approval workflows, the audit trail, and the payroll exports — and watched two days of monthly payroll prep shrink to two hours.

Then we asked the obvious question: how many other teams are stuck in the same place? SyncStaff is the answer — the same battle-tested core, rebuilt as a platform any organization can adopt in an afternoon.

What we believe

  • Employees first. If staff won't use it, it doesn't matter what HR can do with it. Every screen is designed for the person filling it in, not just the person approving it.
  • Trust is a feature. Row-level security, audit logs, and clear permissions aren't enterprise add-ons — they're the foundation, on every plan.
  • The lifecycle is one thing. Hiring, onboarding, managing, and offboarding shouldn't live in four different tools with four different logins.
  • Small teams deserve great software. You shouldn't need an implementation consultant to run HR for 40 people.

The team

We're a product-led team headquartered remotely, led by founder Olatunji Jegede. We ship fast, talk to customers weekly, and publish our roadmap openly.
