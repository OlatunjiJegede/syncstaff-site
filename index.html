The employee lifecycle

From first hello to fond farewell

Most HR tools handle one slice of the journey. SyncStaff connects all of it, so nothing falls through the cracks between stages.

Attract & hire

Job requisitions, a hosted careers page, candidate pipeline, interview scorecards, and offer letters with e-signature.

Onboard

Role-based checklists, document collection, IT provisioning tasks, and a welcome portal — new hires productive on day one.

Manage & grow

Timesheets, leave, expenses, approvals, goals, reviews, and 1-on-1s — with an audit trail behind every decision.

Offboard

Structured exits: asset recovery, access revocation, knowledge transfer, exit interviews, and clean records retention.

Core modules

Everything HR, minus the chaos

Live today: time, leave, expenses, and approvals. Rolling out next: onboarding, ATS, performance, and engagement.

Time & attendance

Weekly or monthly timesheets with billing types, project tracking, overtime rules, and automatic totals.

Leave management

Requests, balances, entitlements, and attachments — with policies for vacation, sick, compassionate, and more.

Expense tracking

Mileage, meals, lodging, and receipts captured per day, routed for approval, and exported for payroll.

Two-stage approvals

Manager review then HR sign-off, with a full audit log of every submit, approve, and reject action.

People directory

Profiles, roles, departments, and manager relationships — synced from Microsoft Entra ID or managed in-app.

Reports & insights

Year-to-date summaries, directory health, approval SLAs, and payroll-ready exports to SharePoint.
