Security

Your people data,
protected by design

HR data is some of the most sensitive data a company holds. Here's exactly how SyncStaff protects it.

Row-level security

Permissions are enforced in the database itself, not just the app. Employees can only read their own records; managers only their reports'; HR and admins per role.

Full audit trail

Every submit, approval, rejection, and role change is written to an append-only audit log with actor, timestamp, and before/after state.

Encryption

TLS 1.2+ for all traffic in transit. AES-256 encryption at rest for the database and file storage (receipts, documents, attachments).

Least-privilege access

Service credentials are never exposed to browsers. Server-side keys are scoped, rotated, and stored in managed secret stores.

Single sign-on

Microsoft Entra ID SSO with single-tenant restriction today; SAML SSO and SCIM provisioning for Enterprise on the roadmap.

Backups & recovery

Automated daily backups with point-in-time recovery on our managed Postgres infrastructure (Supabase).

Infrastructure

SyncStaff runs on Vercel (application hosting, US region) and Supabase (managed PostgreSQL, storage, and authentication). Both providers maintain SOC 2 Type II compliance programs for their infrastructure.

Responsible disclosure

Found a vulnerability? Email security@syncstaff.app. We acknowledge reports within 48 hours and do not pursue action against good-faith research.

Data processing

Enterprise customers can request our Data Processing Agreement, sub-processor list, and security questionnaire responses via sales.
