How it works

Three steps, no training manual

Log the week

Employees enter hours against billing types and projects, drafts save as they type.

Submit for review

Manager sees totals, flags, and history in a split-view inbox built for speed.

HR sign-off

Second-stage approval locks the record and queues it for payroll export.
