Run your people operations now

Timesheets

Weekly and monthly entry with billing types, project links, and automatic totals.

  • Draft autosave, submit when ready
  • Regular, overtime, leave, and sick categories
  • Location tracking per day

Leave management

Requests, approvals, and running balances per leave type and year.

  • Vacation, sick, compassionate, EDO and more
  • Attachments for medical notes
  • Balance auto-updates on approval

Expenses

Per-day expense capture with receipts, routed through approval to payroll.

  • Mileage with from/to logging
  • Meals, lodging, and other categories
  • Receipt photo uploads

Manager review, then HR sign-off — matching real-world chains of responsibility.

  • Split-view approvals inbox
  • Bulk actions for managers
  • Full audit log of every transition

People directory

One source of truth for people, roles, and reporting lines.

  • Employee, manager, HR, finance, admin roles
  • Microsoft Entra ID directory sync
  • Directory health monitoring

Reports

Year-to-date views and payroll-ready exports.

  • YTD hours and expense summaries

  • Approval SLA insights
